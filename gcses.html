Welcome to the GCSEs page, where you can find everything you need to know about the OCR GCSE English conflict cluster, as well as a few links to past papers.

Where do I require poetry?
  • You will need to use your knowledge of the poems from the conflict cluster in order to pass your English Literature GCSE.
  • This requires you to memorise all required poems as well as their meanings and all literary devices employed.
  • However, also keep in mind that using the poetry syllabus is NOT the only part of the GCSE. You'll also need to be able to appreciate and evaluate both classic and modern prose. The qualification is also 100% exam-based.
